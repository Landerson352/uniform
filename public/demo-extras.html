The "even grid" gives you a way to create layouts of evenly sized cells, organized into rows or columns, and it can be nested for a "subdivided" look.

You could use this to create interesting layouts, or simply to create an evenly spaced menu or sequence of images.

You should also notice that the grid can accommodate any amount of content, as each cell will stretch vertically to accomodate it, and thus stretch the entire grid (and all other cells) vertically, in proportion.
